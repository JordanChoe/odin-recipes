Chunky Guacamole

Description

This chunky guacamole has a little kick with the added jalapenos. It is perfect for topping chips, potatoes, or anything you can imagine. You can also eat it straight from the bowl with a fork.
This guacamole is a great complement to the Ribeye with Bleu Cheese and Mushrooms recipe. Use it as a side or topping.

Ingredients

  • Avocados - 2 large, halved, pitted, and diced
  • Tomato - 1, diced
  • Onion - 1/4, chopped
  • Jalapenos - 2, seeded and diced
  • Cilantro - 1 bunch, chopped
  • Lime Juice - 2 TBSP
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste

Instructions

  1. Mash avocado slightly in a bowl.
  2. Add onion, jalapenos, half the diced tomato, cilantro (reserving some for garnish), and lime juice.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Garnish with remaining tomato and cilantro.
  6. Serve.

Enjoy!

Some people prefer a milder guacamole. In this case, simply omit the jalapenos.

To store or chill your guacamole, cover tightly with plastic wrap. Make sure the wrap is touching the guacamole to prevent browning on top. It will still be fine to eat, just less appealing to the eye if you don't.
