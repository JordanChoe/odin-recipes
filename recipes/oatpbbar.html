Oatmeal Peanut Butter Snack Bars

Description

These oatmeal and peanut butter snack bars are simple to make and portable, using only a few ingredients.

Ingredients

This recipe uses ratios. This makes adjustments for personal taste supereasy.

  • Oatmeal - 2 parts
  • Peanut Butter - 1 part
  • Maple Syrup - 1/4 part
  • Raisins, nuts, or chocolate chips - to taste (optional)

In practice, this might look like 2 cups oatmeal, 1 cup peanut butter, and 1/4 cup maple syrup.

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. In a square baking dish, combine oatmeal, peanut butter, and maple syrup.
  3. Spread mixture evenly in pan.
  4. Chill or freeze for 1 hour.
  5. Slice into bars.

Enjoy eating and adjusting the recipe!

In our family, we often skip the slicing portion to just grab some spoons or forks and dig in. These store quite well and do not require refrigeration. If they are not refrigerated, they may become softer and fall apart easier. We will also adjust for taste, and sometimes add other ingredients such as raisins.
