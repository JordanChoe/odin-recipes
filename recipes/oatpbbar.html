Oatmeal Peanut Butter Snack Bars

Description

These oatmeal and peanut butter snack bars are simple to make, use only a few staple ingredients, and portable. Perfect for a quick snack without shopping.

Ingredients

This recipe uses ratios to make adjustments for personal taste super easy.

  • Oatmeal - 2 parts
  • Peanut Butter - 1 part
  • Maple Syrup - 1/4 part
  • Raisins, nuts, or chocolate chips - to taste (optional)

In practice, this might look like 2 cups oatmeal, 1 cup peanut butter, and 1/4 cup maple syrup.

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. In a square baking dish, combine oatmeal, peanut butter, and maple syrup.
  3. Mix in any optional ingredients.
  4. Spread mixture evenly in pan.
  5. Chill or freeze for 1 hour before slicing into bars.

Enjoy eating and adjusting the recipe!

In our family, we often skip the slicing portion to just grab some spoons or forks and dig in. These store quite well without refrigeration. What other mix-ins do you like?
